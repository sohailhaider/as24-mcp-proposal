Beyond APIs: Unleashing Our AI Potential with the Model Context Protocol (MCP)

Standardizing for the Future of AI-Native Interactions

Presented by: Sohail

Date: July 9, 2025

The Challenge: Are We Ready for the Next Wave of AI?

The world of AI is shifting from simple chatbots to autonomous agents. Our current API structure, while robust, creates friction that limits our ability to lead this transformation. We need to evolve to speak the native language of modern AI.

Slow & Costly

Bespoke "glue code" for every new AI integration slows down development and increases costs, hindering our ability to innovate at pace.

Brittle & Fragile

Custom integrations are prone to breaking with API or AI model updates, creating a high maintenance burden for our engineering teams.

Limited Context

AI struggles to maintain a deep, multi-turn understanding across our various services, limiting the intelligence and personalization of its responses.

The Solution: Model Context Protocol (MCP)

MCP is not a replacement for our APIs. It's a universal abstraction layer that sits on top, transforming our existing services into intelligent, AI-consumable "tools" that can be discovered and used dynamically.

Our Proposal: A Phased Approach

We propose a smart, strategic rollout to mitigate risk, demonstrate value quickly, and ensure a smooth transition to an AI-native architecture.

Phase 1: Pilot & Proof of Concept

Q4 2025 - Q1 2026

Focus: Select 2-3 key APIs (e.g., Listing Creation, Market Valuation, **Auto-Catalog**) for MCP exposure.

Goal: Demonstrate seamless AI integration with a leading LLM for a specific dealer workflow.

Deliverable: Internal demo of an AI agent using MCP to generate a new listing from a simple prompt, leveraging comprehensive vehicle data.

Phase 2: Expand & Integrate

H1 2026

Focus: Broaden MCP coverage to more critical APIs (e.g., buyer search, Smyle integration).

Goal: Integrate MCP into existing internal AI tools and explore external partner integrations.

Phase 3: Ecosystem & Evangelism

H2 2026 onwards

Focus: Standardize MCP across all relevant APIs.

Goal: Position AutoScout24 as a leader in AI-native automotive services, fostering a thriving ecosystem around MCP.
